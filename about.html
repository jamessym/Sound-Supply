About Sound Supply

Welcome to Sound Supply, a single location for finding music that suits your preferences. Sound Supply,
developed starting September 2024, enables you to compare and explore various genres, making it simpler
than ever to discover the music you love.

By merely browsing and comparing genres, our platform aims to help music enthusiasts in exploring
further into their favourite sounds and discovering new favourites. Regardless of your musical
preferences—classical, jazz, electronic, or rock—Sound Supply makes it easy and fun to explore
the wide world of music.
